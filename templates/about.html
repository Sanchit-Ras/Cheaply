About Cheaply

Our Mission

Cheaply was created with a simple mission: to help consumers save money on their grocery shopping by providing transparent price comparisons across multiple online platforms.

In today's digital age, consumers have more options than ever when it comes to purchasing groceries online. However, with so many different platforms and retailers, it can be time-consuming and challenging to find the best deals. GroceryCompare solves this problem by aggregating product information and prices from various sources, allowing you to make informed decisions quickly and easily.

Real-time Search

Our platform searches multiple online grocery retailers in real-time to ensure you get the most up-to-date pricing information.

Price Comparison

We normalize prices across different package sizes and units to provide fair and accurate comparisons.

Save Money

By comparing prices across multiple platforms, you can find the best deals and save money on your grocery shopping.

How It Works

  1. Search for Products

    Enter the name of the grocery item you're looking for in the search box.

  2. Compare Prices

    Our system searches multiple online retailers and displays the results in a clear, easy-to-understand format.

  3. Make Informed Decisions

    Compare prices, package sizes, and price per unit to find the best value for your money.

  4. Shop with Confidence

    Click through to the retailer of your choice to complete your purchase, knowing you've found the best deal.
